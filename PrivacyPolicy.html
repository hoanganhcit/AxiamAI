Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

a) Biometric Data: Encrypted facial templates generated during enrollment and authentication. Raw facial images are not stored.
b) Account & Contact Information: Name, email address, username, company affiliation, and billing details.
c) Technical Data: Device identifiers, IP address, browser type, operating system, and activity logs.
d) Usage Data: Authentication logs, timestamps, and session metadata for auditing and compliance.
We use collected information to:
- Provide authentication and identity verification services.
- Maintain and improve system security and performance.
- Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.
- Provide customer support, troubleshooting, and account management.
- Conduct analytics for service optimization.
We process personal and biometric data under these lawful bases:
- Consent: Users provide informed consent to biometric enrollment.
- Contractual Necessity: Data required to provide the Service.
- Legitimate Interests: Security, fraud prevention, and product improvement.
- Legal Obligation: Compliance with applicable data protection laws.
Biometric templates are retained only as long as necessary to provide authentication or as required by contract.
Account and billing data are retained in accordance with financial regulations.
When data is no longer needed, it is securely deleted or anonymized.
We do not sell biometric or personal data. We may share information with:
- Service Providers: Cloud hosting, analytics, and payment processors, bound by confidentiality and data protection obligations.
- Legal Authorities: When required by law, subpoena, or government order.
- Corporate Transactions: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.
We implement industry-standard safeguards, including:
- End-to-end encryption of biometric templates.
- Zero storage of raw facial images.
- Access controls, firewalls, and monitoring.
- Regular security audits and penetration testing.
If data is transferred across borders, we ensure adequate protection via:
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).
- Binding Corporate Rules.
- Other recognized legal mechanisms.
Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to:
- Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
- Withdraw consent for biometric processing.
- Restrict or object to certain processing.
- Data portability (request export of your data).
- File a complaint with a data protection authority.
Requests can be submitted to: [Support Email Address].
The Service is not intended for individuals under 16. We do not knowingly collect biometric or personal data from children.
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on our website, and the “Effective Date” will be updated. Continued use of the Service indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
